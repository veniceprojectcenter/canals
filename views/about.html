HOW IT WORKS

This application displays the canals (blue), canal segments (green) and bridges (red) of the City of Venice. The maps display the location and size, as well as information, on each canal, segment and bridge.

Zoom

Click the PLUS/MINUS on the top-left button to zoom in or out on the map.

Layers

Click LAYERS in the top right to select what infrastructure appears on the map.

Choose CANAL SEGMENTS to see a map with selectable segment, CANALS to see a map with selectable canals, and BRIDGES to see a map with selectable bridges.

Info popup

Click on an infrastructure element to see information specific to that canal, segment, or bridge.

More info

Select MORE INFO to see detailed information on the infrastructure element you have selected.
